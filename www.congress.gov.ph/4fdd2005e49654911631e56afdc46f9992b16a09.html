LEGISLATIVE CALENDAR
SEVENTEENTH CONGRESS, FIRST REGULAR SESSON

Adopted from House Concurrent Resolution No. 6

Introduced by Rep. Rodolfo C. Fari√±as and Rep. Danilo E. Suarez

CONCURRENT RESOLUTION PROVIDING FOR THE LEGISLATIVE CALENDAR FOR THE FIRST REGULAR SESSION OF THE SEVENTEENTH CONGRESS OF THE PHILIPPINES.

RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES, THE SENATE CONCURRING, That the Legislative Calendar for the First Regular Session of the Seventeenth Congress of the Philippines shall be as follows:

 
1. Commencement of Session July 25, 2016 - October 21, 2016
2. Adjournment of Session October 22, 2016 - November 6, 2016
3. Resumption of Session November 7, 2016 - December 16, 2016
4. Adjournment of Session December 17, 2016 - January 15, 2017
5. Resumption of Session January 16, 2017 - March 17, 2017
6. Adjournment of Session March 18, 2017 - May 1, 2017
7. Resumption of Session May 2, 2017 - June 2, 2017
(sine die adjournment)
8. Adjournment of Session June 3, 2017 - July 23, 2017
